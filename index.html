Akwa Ibom State

Akwa Ibom State is a state in the South-South region of Nigeria. It is known for its rich cultural heritage, beautiful beaches, and vibrant economy. The state is home to the Ekpo Abasi shrine and the famous Uquo Ibeno beach. Akwa Ibom State is also known for its agricultural produce, including palm oil and rubber.
